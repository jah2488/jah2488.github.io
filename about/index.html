JUSTIN HERRICK

Software Craftsperson, Game Designer, and Educator

About

Justin is a software craftsperson, educator, Agile advocate, and game designer. He enjoys clean, readable and well-tested code, and believes in iterative development, intuitive user experience and effective design.

Justin understands the power of non-traditional education, and is self taught in Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, among other languages.

Justin resides in Tampa, FL and enjoys tea, coffee, and indie games. He is available for consultation, contract work, or for a friendly conversation.
